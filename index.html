Planned+ Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Overview

Planned+ is a productivity and note-taking application designed to help users create, organize, and manage notes and notebooks.

Information We Collect

Planned+ does not collect, sell, or share personal information.

Local Data Storage

Notes, notebooks, settings, and other app data are stored locally on the user's device. Planned+ does not upload this data to external servers.

Internet Connection

Planned+ does not require an internet connection for core note-taking features.

Third-Party Services

Planned+ does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

Planned+ does not sell, rent, trade, or share user data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Planned+ is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
